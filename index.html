About Us

Every Cyber Security Enthusiast will have a dream to start a new chapter in their career. Unfortunately,many newbies are mislead as to where and how to start. So we came up with the idea of providing a well-designed base platform such that every newcomer would be ready for the industry.

Vision

Our Vision is to make it hassle-free for every Cyber Security enthusiast to kick start their journey in the modern Cyber Era.

Mission

Our Mission is to provide a platform that carves Next Gen Cyber Defenders ready for the Cyber Society.

Who we are

We are a group of Ethical Hackers and Penetraton Testers focused on creating a platform for the new comers in the astonishing field of cyber security and ethical hacking, where they can assess their valuable skills, upskill themselves, and who want to make their carrer in the cyber security field.

What's Our Motto

We are working with a motto “TO HELP BEGINNERS KICK START THEIR CAREER JOURNEY IN THIS CYBER WORLD.”

Resources

Our resources are well-being sources of cognition for enhancing upskilling as part of continuing professional development. Our resources include Networking, Reconnaissance, Vulnerability Assessment (Authenticated & Unauthenticated), Penetration Testing Tools, Exploitation, Risk Analysis, and Reporting. We will provide ‘DISCORD’ authorized access to those who complete all the CTF challenges. These resources we are providing bring a clear view of how cybersecurity attacks will be made and how we need to protect the Cyber Infrastructure from attackers by reporting the security loopholes in the systems.

Labs

We have designed our labs in an efficient way that helps beginners to gain a deeper understanding of hunting vulnerabilities. Our labs cover a vast number of vulnerabilities including OWASP (Open Web Application Security Project) Top 10 vulnerabilities. These labs can make you ready to hunt vulnerabilities on live websites. This journey is paved in such a way that a complete beginner can get the best out of it.

CTF's

Capture The Flag is a security competition that challenges contestants to solve different tasks to find the secrets hidden purposefully. CTFs help to build teamwork and competitive aspects. We have incorporated multi-level CTF challenges in a single window. CTF solver has to finish the challenge within the stipulated time frame mentioned. The one who completes all the CTF levels in the least time will be rewarded with cool SWAGS. Our CTFs can help you to overcome the obstacles that are faced amid your process of bug hunting.
